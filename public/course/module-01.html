Key prompts for this module Prompt 1 — Personal AI Audit
You are helping me conduct a personal AI audit. I work as a [Senior PM / Product Lead] at a [SaaS / enterprise / consumer] company. List the 10 most time-consuming recurring tasks in a typical PM week. For each task, evaluate: (A) Can AI fully automate this today? (B) Can AI significantly reduce the time? (C) Does this require irreplaceable human judgment? Format as a table with a final column showing which skills become MORE valuable as AI handles automatable work.
Prompt 2 — Delegation Stack
I'm building a delegation stack framework for my team's AI adoption. Map these 8 PM activities to one of five levels: [5] Fully AI-autonomous, [4] AI-first PM reviews, [3] AI-assisted PM decides, [2] PM-led AI input, [1] PM only. Activities: market research synthesis, sprint planning, PRD drafting, customer interview analysis, stakeholder presentation prep, metric review, competitive analysis, backlog grooming. Justify each placement with a one-sentence rationale.
