Key prompts for this module Prompt 1 — Production-Grade AI PRD
Write a production-grade AI feature PRD for the following feature: [describe your feature]. Include all of these sections: Executive Summary, Problem Statement with quantified evidence, Success Metrics (including 2 AI-specific eval metrics), User Stories covering the primary flow and 2 edge cases, Technical Requirements for the LLM integration (model, context window, latency budget, fallback behavior), Ethical Considerations and bias risk, Definition of Done. Use specific numbers wherever you'd normally use vague language.
Prompt 2 — Capstone Submission Review
Review my AI Meeting One-Pager: [paste your one-pager]. Score it on these 4 criteria from 1–5: (1) Clarity of human vs. AI roles — does someone reading this immediately understand what AI does and what humans decide? (2) Credibility — would a skeptical CFO find this trustworthy? (3) Actionability — is the recommended first step specific and doable within 30 days? (4) Conciseness — does it make its case in one page? For each score below 4, give one specific improvement.
