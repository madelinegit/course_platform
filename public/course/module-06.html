Key prompts for this module Prompt 1 — Pre-Launch Bias Audit
I'm about to launch an AI feature that [describe feature]. Before launch, I need to run a bias audit. For these 5 user groups: [list groups — e.g., non-native English speakers, users over 60, users in emerging markets, users with accessibility needs, users from different cultural backgrounds]. For each: (1) How might model performance vary? (2) What training data bias could cause this? (3) What's the minimum test we should run before shipping? (4) What's the mitigation if we find a problem? Output as a launch readiness checklist.
Prompt 2 — Transparency Decision
I need to decide whether to disclose AI involvement in this feature: [describe feature]. Arguments for transparency: [list]. Arguments against: [list]. Analyze through three lenses: (1) Long-term user trust — what does research say about disclosed vs. undisclosed AI? (2) Regulatory risk — does the EU AI Act or CCPA require disclosure in this case? (3) Competitive positioning — does disclosure help or hurt? Give me a recommendation with your confidence level and the one factor that would change your advice.
