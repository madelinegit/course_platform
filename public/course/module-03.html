Key prompts for this module Prompt 1 — Roadmap Stress-Test
Here is my current Q[N] roadmap: [paste your initiatives]. Act as a skeptical CPO who has seen too many AI-generated roadmaps. For each initiative, challenge me with: (1) What evidence supports this being the right priority right now? (2) What are we NOT doing because we're doing this? (3) If a competitor ships this first, what happens? Don't soften the feedback — I need to defend this in a board meeting.
Prompt 2 — Exec Communication
I need to communicate this roadmap decision to a skeptical executive team: [describe the decision]. The most likely objections are: [list 2–3]. Write a 5-minute verbal narrative that: opens with the business outcome at stake (not the feature), explains the AI analysis that surfaced this priority, directly addresses each objection with data, and closes with a specific ask. Use plain language — no PM jargon.
