Key prompts for this module Prompt 1 — Interview Synthesis
I have the following customer feedback from 3 sources: [support tickets], [NPS survey responses], [interview transcripts]. Analyze all three for: (1) Top 5 pain points ranked by frequency and emotional intensity, (2) Jobs-to-be-done statements extracted from the language customers actually use, (3) One insight that seems to contradict the surface-level pattern — what might we be misreading? Format as an executive summary with a "watch out" section at the end.
Prompt 2 — Competitive Intelligence
Act as a competitive intelligence analyst. Product category: [describe your category]. Research question: Where is the market underserving customers that AI-powered features could address in the next 12 months? Analyze the top 4 competitors. For each: current AI features, biggest user complaints on review sites, and the specific gap that creates an opportunity. Conclude with a ranked list of the 3 highest-conviction opportunities.
