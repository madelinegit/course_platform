Key prompts for this module Prompt 1 — Mixed-Audience AI Presentation
I need to present our AI strategy to a mixed audience: some are excited, some deeply skeptical, one is convinced AI is a fad. I have 10 minutes. Write a presentation structure that: (1) Opens with something the skeptics cannot dismiss, (2) Gives the enthusiasts the vision without losing the skeptics, (3) Acknowledges real AI risks (which builds credibility), (4) Ends with a concrete, low-risk first step that everyone can support. Include 3 specific data points I can reference to establish credibility.
Prompt 2 — Public Failure Response
Our AI feature [feature name] failed in the following way: [describe the failure]. I need two documents: (1) Internal post-mortem for the engineering and product team — what failed, why, what we're changing, and how we'll prevent it. Be completely honest. (2) External response — a public-facing statement that's honest about what happened without creating legal liability or making the failure sound worse than it is. Keep the external version under 200 words.
